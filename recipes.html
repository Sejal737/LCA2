Berry Blast Bowl

Ingredients: 1 banana, 1/2 cup strawberries, 1/2 cup blueberries, 1/4 cup coconut milk, granola, chia seeds.

Instructions: Blend the banana, berries, and coconut milk until smooth. Pour into a bowl and top with granola and chia seeds.

Tropical Mango Bowl

Ingredients: 1 cup mango chunks, 1/2 banana, 1/2 cup pineapple, 1/4 cup coconut water, shredded coconut.

Instructions: Blend all fruits with coconut water until creamy. Top with shredded coconut and fresh fruits.

Green Detox Bowl

Ingredients: 1/2 apple, handful spinach, 1 kiwi, 1 banana, 1/2 cup almond milk, chia seeds.

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Pour into a bowl and top with chia seeds and sliced kiwi.

Chocolate Banana Bowl

Ingredients: 1 banana, 1 tbsp cocoa powder, 1 tbsp almond butter, 1/2 cup oat milk, granola, dark chocolate chips.

Instructions: Blend banana, cocoa, almond butter, and oat milk. Pour and top with granola and chocolate chips.
